Our Story

More Than Just Coffee

Brewhaven started in 2016 as a single cart in a farmers' market, powered by one espresso machine and a lot of stubborn optimism. Today, we're a full café — but the mission hasn't changed.

We still roast in small batches, still greet regulars by name, and still believe a great cup of coffee can turn an ordinary morning into something worth savoring.
