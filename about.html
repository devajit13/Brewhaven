More Than Just Coffee

Brewhaven started as a single cart at a Saturday farmers' market, run by one espresso machine, one folding table, and a lot of stubborn optimism. Our founder, then a part-time barista with a full-time obsession, wanted to prove that a neighborhood coffee cart could hold its own against any downtown café.

Word spread fast. Within a year, the cart had a line out the tent every Saturday morning. By 2018, we'd opened our first storefront on Roastery Lane — the same location we call home today.

What hasn't changed since that first folding table: every bean is sourced directly from farmers at fair prices, every batch is roasted in-house, and every drink is made by someone who genuinely loves the craft. We're not trying to be the biggest coffee shop in town — just the one you actually want to walk into.
